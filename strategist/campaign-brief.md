---
name: campaign-brief
description: Write a creative brief for a campaign. Defines objective, target avatar, awareness level, messaging angle, proof requirements, and channels. Use before any copy production.
---

Write a campaign brief for $ARGUMENTS.

1. Read dossier + avatars + learnings for the client
2. Define:
   - Campaign objective (awareness / leads / conversions)
   - Target avatar(s)
   - Primary awareness level to target (from dossier Section 4)
   - Sophistication stage (from dossier Section 5) — determines messaging approach
   - Emotional anchor (primary emotion to lead with)
   - Proof requirements (what evidence buyers demand at this stage)
   - Channels (where these buyers are)
   - 3 headline directions (grounded in dossier verbatim phrases)
   - Forbidden angles (from learnings.md saturated list)
3. HITL gate: present brief for human approval

Output: clients/<project>/campaigns/<campaign>/brief.md
DM Copywriter with brief path after approval.
