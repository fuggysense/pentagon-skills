---
name: email-sequence
description: Create email drip campaigns and nurture sequences. Welcome series, re-engagement, lead nurture. Grounded in buyer awareness level from dossier.
---

Create an email sequence for $ARGUMENTS.

1. Read dossier to understand buyer awareness level
2. Map sequence to awareness journey: unaware → problem-aware → solution-aware → product-aware
3. Per email: subject line (2 variations), preview text, body, CTA
4. Use dossier Power Words in subject lines and CTAs
5. Each email: 150-300 words max, mobile-first formatting
6. Include send timing recommendations (day + time)

Output: clients/<project>/campaigns/<campaign>/emails/sequence.md

Sequence types:
- Welcome (5-7 emails over 14 days)
- Nurture (7-10 emails over 30 days)
- Re-engage (3-5 emails over 10 days)
